We all have a right to a secret identity.

Join the Electronic Frontier Foundation, io9 and a coalition of fan communities for Project Secret Identity, a cosplay photo campaign to raise awareness of how anonymity and privacy are key to free expression.

Add your photo. Demand freedom from government surveillance when you use technology.

Two ways to participate.

1. Join Us at Dragon Con. During Dragon Con in Atlanta, Aug. 29 – Sept. 1, you can also visit our photo stations at the convention: EFF (Table 7 at the Hilton) or Southeastern Browncoats (Table #1000 at AmericasMart).

2. Join the campaign from anywhere in the world. Can't make it to Dragon Con? Join anyway by uploading your photo using the button below.
