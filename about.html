About

From George Orwell's Big Brother to J.K. Rowling's Ministry of Magic, science fiction, fantasy and other genre fiction have long explored and criticized the intrusion of government on our private lives.

Today, many of those fictions have become reality, whether that's NSA mass surveillance, local police use of spy tech, or big data brokers scraping from social media networks. Some governments are even trying to ban online anonymity.

Project Secret Identity underlines the belief that we must protect and advocate for ourselves in order to shape the future.

Please join EFF, io9, Southeastern Browncoats, Harry Potter Alliance, Baker Street Babes, Wattpad and the Organization for Transformative Works for this cosplay activism campaign during Dragon Con (Aug. 29 – Sept 1) in Atlanta, Georgia.

You don't need to be at Dragon Con to participate: just upload your photos here. If you are at the convention, please stop by one of our two photo stations: EFF (Table 7 at the Hilton) or Southeastern Browncoats (Table TK at TK).
